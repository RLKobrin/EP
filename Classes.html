Biomechanics

BME345 Biomedical Solid Mechanics

Biomedical Solid Mechanics built upon the foundation of BME205 Intro to Biomedical Mechanics by introducing non-static systems. The class focused on both solids and dynamics, with topics including kinematics, kinetics, finite element analysis, and strain/stress transformations. Class projects included the construction of a MATLAB model of the forces and kinetics of a biker (found in the Code section).

Skills: MATLAB, Kinetics, Kinematics, FEA, Solids
